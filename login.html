Finacy Privacy Policy

This policy explains exactly what Finacy stores, how it is protected, and how you control it.

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Local storage of business records

Finacy stores your inventory, sales, rental records, and related app content locally in your browser.

  • Business records remain on your device by default.
  • Records are not automatically uploaded to external servers by dashboard storage logic.
  • Data transfers happen only when you explicitly export or import files.
  • Authentication traffic is encrypted in transit.
  • Finacy does not store your password in plain text.
  • Account verification is separate from local business-record storage.

Encryption and technical safeguards

  • HTTPS/TLS protects network traffic used for login and service communication.
  • Browser storage is sandboxed per origin and subject to browser security controls.
  • Device-level protection (for example full-disk encryption and OS account security) remains user-managed.
  • Finacy is designed to minimize data exposure.

5. Your control over information

  • Use dashboard Settings tools to export text and image data.
  • Import tools let you restore previously exported records.
  • You can remove local records by clearing this site's browser data.

6. Data protection principles

Finacy follows core principles often required by modern privacy regulations, including transparency, minimization, purpose limitation, user access, and deletion control. These controls are intended to align with GDPR and CCPA-style expectations for user trust and informed consent.
